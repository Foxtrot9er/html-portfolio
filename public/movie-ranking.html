The Best Movies According to me!

My 3 favorite movies of all-time:

Gladiator

I love this film due to a combination of the briliant acting performances from each of the actors, and the captivating music!

Avatar

This film makes my list thanks to the absolutely STUNNING visuals, which are so rich, they manage to convey the viewer to the spectacular world of Pandora!

Titanic

When I watch this film, I am so captured by the story of the elegent ship that was, as well as the moving and brilliant romance that unfurls before, and amidst the historic tragidy. I become a passenger of the film.
